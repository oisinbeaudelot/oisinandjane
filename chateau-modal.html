A Note About Dining
Kindly note that the Château does not offer a bar or dining facilities for lunch or dinner outside of the wedding festivities.

Nevertheless, we invite you to discover the exquisite dining options available in Ancenis, all within a convenient driving distance. For those craving delectable pizza, we recommend Le comptoir à pizza, located a mere 5km away—personally vetted and endorsed by the bride and groom. Additionally, guests are most welcome to bring whichever refreshments and culinary delights take their fancy to enjoy during their stay at the Château.
