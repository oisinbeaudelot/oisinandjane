The Destination

Le Château de la Colaissière
37 Rue de Calvaire, 49270 Orée-d'Anjou, FRANCE

Nestled in the hidden scapes of the Loire Valley, We are delighted to welcome you to the stunning grounds of the Château de la colaissière. Located in the heart of the picturesque Orée-d'Anjou, the château boasts a comfortable courtyard, dining hall, all surrounded by a serene moat.

Accommodation

 

We’ll be in touch re: accommodation once all RSVPs have been received. The chateau can accommodate up to 44 people on the eve of the wedding and the wedding night. We are also coordinating a list of nearby hotels, from which a shuttle bus will run to the venue on the wedding day. The chateau will be booked through the couple, the hotel will be booked by the guests. More details to follow once all RSVPs have been received.
